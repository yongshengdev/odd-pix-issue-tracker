# Privacy Policy for OddPix

**Last Updated: April 19, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how OddPix ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1. Local Use and On-Device Data

OddPix is designed so that **core features do not require an internet connection**. The App processes your content and settings on your device. Any preferences or data you save for convenience (for example, app settings) are stored **locally on your device** and are not sent to our servers, because **we do not operate backend servers for the App**.

### 1.2. In-App Purchases (Apple)

We offer a **one-time** in-app purchase for **USD $1.00** to unlock paid features. Purchases are processed **only** through **Apple’s In-App Purchase system**. We do **not** use third-party subscription or analytics services for purchases.

- **Data involved**: Apple handles payment and may collect information related to the transaction in line with [Apple’s Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- **Our access**: We do not receive your full payment card details. Apple provides us with the information needed to confirm that you have purchased the unlock (for example, transaction or entitlement status as made available by Apple).

### 1.3. Personal Information

We do not knowingly collect, store, or share personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location through the App itself. If you email us (see below), we will use your message only to respond to you.

## 2. Data Security

We prioritize keeping your information on your device under your control. Data that remains on the device is subject to your device’s own security and backup settings.

## 3. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page or within the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
